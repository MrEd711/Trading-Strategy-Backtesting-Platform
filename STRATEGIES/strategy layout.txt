TJR Trading Strat Simplified

Assumptions:

- No slippage 
- No trading fees
- Instantaneous buying and selling

Strategy:

- Calculate the simple moving average.
- If below then only buying
- If above then only selling

- Identify 3 candle fair value gap in the correct direction
- or 3 candle inverse fair value gap in the correct direction
- RR 1:2, with the 2 in the predicted direction
- The reward section should be 2/3rds of the way from the long/short entry to the SMA
